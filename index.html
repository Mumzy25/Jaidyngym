On-Site Meal Prep Guide

FIFO Nutrition Tips

🥩
Prioritise protein at every mealAsk for double protein at the mess. Grilled chicken, beef, fish, eggs — always first on your plate.
🥤
Protein shake timingHave one immediately after training and one before bed. Pack your own powder — sites often have limited options.
📦
Pack these in your bagProtein powder, creatine, mixed nuts, beef jerky, protein bars. These fill gaps when the mess is limited.
💧
Hydration on siteAim for 3-4L per day. Heat and physical work increase needs significantly. Electrolytes help.
🍳
Breakfast is your biggest winMess breakfast usually has eggs, meat, and good protein options. Load up — it sets your protein base for the day.
🌙
Evening snackCottage cheese or Greek yogurt before bed supports overnight muscle recovery. Check the mess fridge.
